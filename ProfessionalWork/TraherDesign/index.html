Traher Design | Freelance Designer

2008 to 2013 — Elissa Traher, Principle Designer

Over the past several years I’ve been a contract designer for Traher Design, a boutique design studio located in historic Lexington, MA. The principle designer, Elissa, hired me for my expertise in web and ability to nail tough designs. Over the years I’ve worked on a number of projects for Traher Design, most notably the design and build of their portfolio website. To this day I still do periodic work for Traher Design.
