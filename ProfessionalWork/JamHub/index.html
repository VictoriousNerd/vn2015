JamHub | Senior Designer

2012 to 2014

JamHub is a small start up in the music equipment industry. They specialize in unique technological solutions to allow musicians to play music together more often. Their flagship products, the JamHub studios, allow multiple musicians to connect to a single device and with personal headphone mixes for each musician. While at JamHub I created a vast library of work in their marketing department and in product and app development. A few of the most notable projects are here.

JamHub website

Corporate website showcasing the company's products and information.

BandLab Beta

Sole UI designer for Beta version of BandLab app for iPads and iPhones.

Tracker MT16 box

I designed the first production run of the Tracker MT16 box, product silkscreen graphics, and product manual.

JamHub Ads

Designed a variety of print and digital ads for the company.

Tradeshow graphics, giveaways, and educational documents incorporating the companies branding.
