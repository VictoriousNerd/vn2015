Frequently Asked Questions

What is LitBoard?

LitBoard is a mobile app designed to give you complete access to your content library with the most up-to-date sales and marketing materials at your fingertips.

How do I get started?

It’s easy. Just contact one of our team members at info@litboardstudios.com. We’ll set up a demo and take you through the simple implementation process.

How can I customize and connect LitBoard to my current apps?

LitBoard provides an API key that will allow you to connect your LitBoard to other apps and even customize certain aspects of your Litboard.

Can it be branded with my logo in the App Store?

Yes! You can white label LitBoard to comply with your business’ branding guidelines.
