MobileFuse | Mobile Designer

2014 to 2015

MobileFuse is a mobile advertising services company based in New York, NY and Waltham, MA. MobileFuse's specialties are the metrics, distribution, and ad creation with a focus on mobile environments. While at MobileFuse I was a User Interface and User Experience Designer as well as an Mobile Advertising Designer. On the UI/UX side I created mocks for internal metrics software and proposed vendor metrics software. On the advertising side I created Landing Pages that were desktop and mobile friendly, as well as a variety of Rich Media Ads for smartphones and tablets.
