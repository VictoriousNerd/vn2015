Video Games Live Event Posters

2006 & 2012 — College project re-design
Janine Wong, Instructor

This assignment required taking a real life event and creating a poster series for it. Video Games Live ties orchestral music with moments from famous video games. The main purpose of this project was to create a cohesive series that tied together and represented the event well. In 2012 I revisited and updated the pieces.

For more information on Video Games Live visit their site at www.videogameslive.com.
