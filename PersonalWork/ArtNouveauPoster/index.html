Education (Art Nouveau Inspired) Poster

2008 — Anna Dempsey, Instructor

The "Education" poster is based off the Art Nouveau movement; steeped in symbolism and organic shapes, yet re-drawn in Illustrator to give a graphic feel. This assignment for my History of Design class was to create a poster in a modern fashion that represented an older movement.
